# Customer And Geography Map — HCG

HealthCare Global Enterprises Limited (NSE: HCG / BSE: 539787) — India-headquartered cancer-care hospital network (comprehensive cancer centres, 3 multispecialty hospitals, and the "Milann" fertility chain). "Customers" here are patients; "payers" are who actually settles the bill. Authoritative figures are from the FY24-25 Integrated Annual Report (audited, year ended 31 Mar 2025); the most recent period (FY26, year ended 31 Mar 2026) is covered only by the investor deck and earnings transcript dated May 2026 (the FY26 audited annual report is not yet in the data pool).

## 1. Customer Map

HCG does not have named B2B customers. Revenue comes from a widespread base of individual patients, classified by who pays. The audited financial statements state plainly that no single customer is material. The Capital IQ "Customers" export in the pool contains no named customer data, consistent with a patient-funded hospital model.

## 4. Read

HCG is concentrated on geography, not on customers. On the customer side it is the opposite of concentrated — no single customer exceeds 10% of revenue (audited, both consolidated and standalone, FY25 and FY24), because revenue is millions of individual cancer/fertility patients. The real concentration is geographic: ~98% of revenue is earned in one country (India), and within India four state groupings — Karnataka 30%, Gujarat+Rajasthan 28%, AP+East 17%, Maharashtra 14% — account for ~89% of FY25 revenue, with the only overseas presence a single Kenya centre at roughly 2%. This concentration is naked, not contractually secured: hospital revenue is per-episode and walk-in/referral driven, with uninsured patients settling in cash on discharge and no long-term volume contracts disclosed. The single biggest dependency the synthesizer should carry forward is HCG's near-total reliance on the Indian market, sharpened by a ~33% revenue dependence on government health-scheme payers (FY26) whose tariffs and payment cycles HCG does not control.

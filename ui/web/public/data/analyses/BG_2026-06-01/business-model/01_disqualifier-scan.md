## 2. Triggered Disqualifiers — Detail

No disqualifier triggered.

Additional context (not a trigger, flagged for the synthesizer): The FY2025 auditor's report carried two critical audit matters — (a) fair value of readily marketable inventories and physically settled forward contracts, driven by Level 3 unobservable inputs in Brazil, and (b) goodwill for the Grain Merchandising reporting unit, whose fair value exceeded carrying value by only 9% at the measurement date (total goodwill $3,141M; $593M allocated to Grain Merchandising) [FY25 10-K, p. F-2 / lines 4318–4375]. Critical audit matters are not opinion qualifications and do not trigger Disqualifier 1; the opinion remained unqualified. Separately, management excluded the newly acquired Viterra operations (33% of total assets ex-goodwill, 22% of net sales) from its FY2025 internal-control-over-financial-reporting assessment under SEC first-year guidance [FY25 10-K, Item 9A, lines 3683–3687]. This is a permitted exclusion, not a control deficiency, and does not trigger any disqualifier — but it is a control-scope gap worth monitoring in the next cycle.

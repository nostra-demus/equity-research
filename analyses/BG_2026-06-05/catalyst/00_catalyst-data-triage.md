- US-China trade/tariff resolution: open-ended, no date.

The most concrete scheduled event found is the **3.25% Senior Notes ~$700M maturity sometime in 2026**, which is the largest remaining hard-dated debt obligation inside 12 months (the 2.00% notes were already repaid April 21, 2026) — though the exact coupon/maturity date is not stated in the 10-Q text. The second-most concrete is the **Q2 2026 earnings event in ~late July 2026** with a fully populated beat/miss setup from the earnings module.

The calendar will carry a mix of proven near-term events (results, active buyback, dividend schedule, debt maturity horizon) and several soft-window or undated catalysts (Destrehan commissioning, trade/tariff resolution, EPA RVO update, lock-up expiry, rating-agency action). It is not a thematic-only story, but it is also not a clean, all-dated calendar. The biggest data gap for upgrading to Sufficient is the Destrehan commissioning date and the Viterra seller lock-up expiry date — neither is disclosed in the pool.

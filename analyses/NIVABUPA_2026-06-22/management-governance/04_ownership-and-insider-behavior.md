## 5. Ownership / Insider Read

Management holds approximately 0.88% of shares — a minimal stake, with no evidence of open-market buying from any available source. All available data is consistent with this stake being accumulated through ESOPs or grants rather than cash purchase, though the primary SEBI LODR filing (which would confirm this) is absent. The two institutional promoters — Bupa Group (UK) at 55.35% and Fettle Tone / Fairfax at ~7.91% — hold a combined ~63.3% and control the company outright; Bupa's long-term objective (building a scaled India health insurer) is broadly aligned with minority holders, but the structurally unverifiable risk of RPT value leakage through Bupa brand, technology, or reinsurance fees prevents a clean sign-off on promoter alignment. The net insider signal is neutral: zero transactions, zero pledging disclosed, zero promoter bloc changes in the most recent window — and an institutionally net-buying market (16 new or increased institutional positions vs 4 reduced), with Franklin Resources making the largest single add.

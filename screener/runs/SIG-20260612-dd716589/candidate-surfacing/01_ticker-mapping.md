## 4. Verdict

Verdict: 7 candidates across 6 parties (5 long / 1 short, plus 1 unmappable party and 1 unmappable short)

**Biggest finding:** Flex LNG (NYSE: FLNG) is the purest expression of the thesis variant — the only stock where the specific charter-renewal rate reset mechanism (the coverage gap confirmed by five documented searches) directly adds forward EBITDA above the current sell-side model, yet the entire sell-side consensus is Sell at $24.92 vs. a $29.48 current price, creating a short-squeeze overhang if the Q2 2026 earnings or any new charter announcement forces a model update.

**Pair trade status:** The Long FLNG / Short LNG-importer pair suggested in M0.3 has no liquid short leg in listed equities. The closest available pair is Long FLNG (shipping rate reset) / Short ENEL (gas-cost squeeze in Italian CCGT generation), but the ENEL short is poorly quantified and the diversification of ENEL's business means the thesis impact is diluted. The research swarm should size the ENEL short leg carefully or treat it as a hedge rather than a primary short.

**Geography note:** This thesis is global but the blast radius is Europe-facing (TTF/storage) and US/Australia LNG supply; no India-listed company maps to any carry-forward party. NSE/BSE names are not present because no Indian company operates LNG liquefaction, ocean-going LNG carrier fleets, or European gas storage infrastructure.

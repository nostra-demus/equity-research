## 1. What Happened (3 lines max)

Honeywell International's Board of Directors formally approved the spin-off of Honeywell Aerospace, with distribution set for June 29, 2026. Existing shareholders receive one share of Honeywell Aerospace (to trade as "HONA") for every two Honeywell shares held as of the June 15, 2026 record date. After the spin-off, the remaining company rebrands as Honeywell Technologies ("HON") and immediately executes a 1-for-2 reverse stock split.
